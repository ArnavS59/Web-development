Here we go..

This website is a guide into acing your ics module exam. If you go through this webpage Iam pretty sure you will have a good idea as to what to expect in the final exam.

Note: this by no means is an offical page/guide instead it is the work of a second year student and his desire to share his knowledge and expierience.

So let's get started

A little description about whats going on


So first of all one of the problems I faced in the begging of this course was the lectures,I understood more by self-studying and the tutorial sessions then I did by the lectures. This is not to say the lectures are fruitless. Go attend the lectures, since you'll have a rough idea whats going on otherwise you'll be blank. What helped later, was reading the course slides before going to class, (you don't have to understand everything) but it was a bit helpful in the sense that in class since I knew atleast what was going on. I won't get into to the technicalities of the module. It's written clearly here, every week your given a homework assignment and is related to what you studied in class, and you need 50% to be able to write the exam.


Here is the takeway:

  • Just skim over or read before going to class if you have the time
  • Go to the lectures even though you don't understand shit
  • Self-study and make notes of your doubts try solving the homework
  • Must: Definitly go to the tutorial session's held by the TA's and ask your doubts.
  • Come back try solving the questions again
  • Discuss with your friends if your stuck
  • File it in your folder once done, you'll need for revising

Thanks for reading this if you found this helpful please share this with your friends so they can benefit as well.
