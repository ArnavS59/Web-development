Important topics to look at before writing the exam

# Topics to definitely go through Most probable type of Question Comments
1 Boyer Moore-Algorithm 10 marks or MCQ Could be asked to apply BMA to a text or it could come as a true, false Question Make sure you know how to apply the good-suffix all 3 cases, bad character rules.
2 Kruskal- Maze generation Algo MCQ, theoritcal question You just need to understand what it , no computation required
3 Definitions of different types of Algo MCQ, short answer Difference between total and partial correctness
4 Ways of proofing 10 marks Could be asked to prove something by proof of induction/contradiction
5 Understand the properties of binary realtions Medium 10 mark or true false Might need to apply the different properties eg. reflexiv, transitive etc to an example
6 Understand the properties of a Function Medium 10 mark or true false Might be asked show that the given function is surjective but not injective or something along those lines. You to know the difference between all three
7 Conversions from one base to another Short 2-3 mark question YOu need to know how to convert from one base to another so like 123 in octal to binary vice versa etc, Also b-complement is important , remember the formula
8 Floating point decimal conversion Long 10-20 marks The whole methodlogy of solving should be remembered by heart, mantissa, sign bit, bias etc.
9 Date, time , character encoding, derived vs SI units Short 5 marks Brief understanding on how clocks synchronize, timezone offsets and different types of character encoding UTF-8, ASCII
10 Differnet boolean truth tables Short 5 marks Write these down into ur cheat sheet and memorize the logic gate symbols, know how to make a truth table for a given boolean expression
11 Solve/Simplify Boolean Expression using the laws Short 5-10 marks You need to be able to simply a given bool expression usinig the laws, like distributive, de morgan etc..
12 Obtain CNF DNF from a truth table Short 2 marks Know the difference between the two and how to get the CNF/DNF from a truth table
13 Quine McKlusky Algo Long 20 Marks Know the methodology for solving the QMA for 4 variables , implicants, prime implicants,cost
14 Logic Gates and Adders Medium 10 Marks You might be given a logic gate diagram and asked to interpret it, so write the turth table formulate expressions etc I would suggest you to copy the truth tables, diagrams etc for all adders, full, half, carry
15 Sequential Gates using NAND and NOR Gates Medium 10 Marks You might be given a logic gate diagram like SR or Master Slave and asked to interpret it, or draw the time delay diagram. I would suggest you to copy the truth tables, diagrams etc and understand how the SR-latch and master slave wors ks
15 Von Neumann Architecture Short or True Flase Theortical quesition based on VNA
16 Machine Code language Long 10-15 marks You would be given a machine code and asked to translate it into c/c++ and brifely explain what it is doing
17 BNF form, compiler Short 5-10 marks KNow the differnce betweeen a compiler adn ann interpreter and the steps of a compiler, how to get a BNF form
18 Forking and Pid diagram 5-10 marks Be able to draw a pid diagram or explain the forking process
18 Software Corecctness 10 marks Know the correctness laws
